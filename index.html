Our Beginning

An American owned entity since inception, WMT Precision LLC has been in existance since March of 2018, but our history dates back to the 1970's.

In 1972 our company was created and run by a gent named "Bucky" Weaver. Bucky was an avid believer in a strong and strict company mode of operation. Although we are a different company in many ways, we have modeled ourselves after Bucky's vision. Customer Satisfaction was and always will be a key element in our strategy.

We believe we achieve Customer Satisfaction on many levels; KPI such as 100% OTD, Zero Defect Quality, and Pricing that is competitive in the market. We like to think we take Customer Satisfaction to another level with communication. Customer Intimacy is our objective.

Our Transformation

Building on our foundation, we have embarked on the Continuous Improvement "journey". We have been stepping through the many facets of CI.

The tool box is full and we are using many of the tools available to us. We are training our entire team to enhance our understanding of quality concepts like SPC and Perato charts and CI through Lean concepts like 6S, SMED and Takt Time. These concepts will enhance our ability to serve our customer base better.

More importantly, it unifies and grounds us all to a common structure. We are grounded in the fundamentals of a successful organization.

Our Future

Our future is very promising. We will remain true to our core beliefs as we take the business to new levels. We have a growth strategy that includes new equipment purchases, manning increases, customer base growth, and facility expansion.

Our facility requires that we improve our asset base to include state of the art equipment. With that investment comes the need for an increased level of machinist capabilities. The two key assets go hand-in-hand. We plan to meter in change, monitor the gaps and set a plan to close the gaps.

Our future is planned, but we are open to change and embrace the opportunity to take our operation to new places.

COVID-19 Update

To our valued Customer(s), Suppliers(s) et al:

WMT Precision LLC has been granted Essential Business status. We will be complying with every protocol issued by NYS from reduction of non-essential employees working from home to every precaution internally to comply with the three primary activities; wash your hands and stay socially distant and if within 6’ wear a mask. We resumed operations as soon as we received Essential Business status. We have established new operating patterns to allow for social distancing. Our operation, during normal run time, allows for social distancing, but our Breaks and Lunch times required a shift in mindset and location. Our modifications are as follows:

  • We have separated our two shifts. We will be shifting the 1st shift operations one half hour earlier to allow for a gap in contact at the transition from 1st to 2nd shift.
  • Breaks and Lunches have been arranged such that the folks will not be within 6’ of each other.
  • We are suspending use of all vending machines to minimize contact points.
  • Similar to the protocol at a few businesses in the area, we will be asking employees to ask themselves (4) simple questions prior to leaving for work. If an individual answers “YES” to any, they are requested to remain home.This protocol also applies to any visitor wishing to enter our facility. If any question receives a positive response, they will not be allowed in the facility.

Our efforts are aimed to keep our folks safe while they are in our facility. We feel these actions will also minimize any latent effect on our customer base as well. We are optimistic that our precautions will be effective as intended. We wish you all well and be safe and socially responsible.

Please call with any questions.
